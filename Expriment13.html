Healthy Fruits

Fruits are an important part of a healthy diet. They provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber required for the human body.

Seasonal fruits are fresh, tasty, and more nutritious.

Types of Fruits

  • Apple – Rich in fiber and vitamins
  • Banana – Good source of energy
  • Orange – High in Vitamin C
  • Mango – Known as the king of fruits

Eating fruits daily helps improve immunity and overall health.
