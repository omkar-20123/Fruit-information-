♦ About Fruits ♦

          Fruits are natural food items that are rich in vitamins, minerals, and fiber. They are an essential part of a healthy diet and help in maintaining good health. Fruits provide energy, improve digestion, and boost immunity. Different fruits are available in different seasons and each fruit has its own nutritional value. Eating fresh fruits daily helps prevent many diseases and keeps the body fit and active.
