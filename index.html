Fruit Introduction

Fruit is an important part of a healthy diet and is enjoyed by people all around the world. Fruits come from plants and trees and are usually sweet or slightly sour in taste. They provide essential vitamins, minerals, and fiber that help our bodies grow, stay strong, and fight illness. Because fruits come in many colors, shapes, and flavors, they are both nutritious and enjoyable to eat.
